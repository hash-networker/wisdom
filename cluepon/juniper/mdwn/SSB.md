[thumb|right|System Switch Board (SSB)](/Image:SSB_Front.jpg "wikilink")

The System Switch Board (SSB) is the switch board for M20 router only. The SSB and SCB are the only two boards to ever ship with the original [Internet Processor](/Internet_Processor "wikilink") ASIC. The part number "SSB" refers to the original IP1 board, while the part number "SSB-E" refers to the [Internet Processor II](/Internet_Processor_II "wikilink") version. The SSB-E also says "Internet Processor II" on the front right of the faceplate, while the original SSB version is blank in this area.
